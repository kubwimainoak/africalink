Personnel Provision Services

Access to qualified captains and crew members to ensure your maritime operations run smoothly

Our Personnel Provision Services

At Africa Link, we understand that the success of your maritime operations depends on having the right personnel at the helm. Our Personnel Provision services connect you with highly qualified captains, officers, and crew members who bring expertise, experience, and professionalism to your vessels.

Whether you need to fill temporary vacancies, assemble a complete crew, or find specialized maritime professionals, our extensive network of vetted maritime personnel ensures you have access to the talent you need, when you need it.

Maritime Personnel We Provide

Officers

We provide access to experienced and certified officers for your vessel:

  • Captains/Masters with extensive command experience
  • Chief Officers/First Mates
  • Second and Third Officers
  • Chief Engineers with comprehensive technical knowledge
  • First, Second, and Third Engineers
  • Electrical Officers and Technical Officers

Deck Crew

Our deck crew members are skilled and trained to handle various vessel operations:

  • Bosuns with leadership and operational experience
  • Able Seamen (AB) qualified in seamanship duties
  • Ordinary Seamen (OS) for general deck support
  • Deckhands experienced in various vessel types
  • Riggers specialized in offshore operations
  • Crane Operators for specialized cargo handling

Engine Crew

Our engine department personnel ensure the efficient operation of your vessel's propulsion systems:

  • Motormen with mechanical expertise
  • Oilers skilled in machinery maintenance
  • Fitters trained in repair and fabrication
  • Electricians for electrical system maintenance
  • Engine Room Attendants for general support
  • Refrigeration Engineers for specialized systems

Hospitality Staff

We provide quality hospitality staff to ensure comfort aboard your vessels:

  • Chief Cooks experienced in maritime galleys
  • Second Cooks and Assistant Cooks
  • Stewards for cabin and common area service
  • Messmen for dining service and support
  • Laundry Personnel for maintenance of linens and uniforms
  • Medical Personnel for health and safety support

Specialized Personnel

We also provide specialized maritime personnel for specific operations:

  • Dynamic Positioning Officers (DPOs) for specialized vessels
  • ROV (Remotely Operated Vehicle) Technicians
  • Offshore Installation Managers
  • HSE (Health, Safety, Environment) Officers
  • Maritime Security Personnel
  • Surveyors for specialized inspection services

Benefits of Our Personnel Provision Services

Qualified Personnel

All personnel undergo rigorous screening, including verification of certifications, experience, and references to ensure they meet international maritime standards.

Quick Response

Our extensive network allows us to respond rapidly to your personnel needs, even on short notice or in emergency situations.

Local Knowledge

Many of our personnel have specific experience working in African waters and ports, bringing valuable local knowledge to your operations.

Flexibility

We offer flexible staffing solutions, from single personnel placements to complete crew provision, on both short-term and long-term contracts.

Our Recruitment Process

1Needs Assessment

We begin by thoroughly understanding your specific personnel requirements, including qualifications, experience levels, contract duration, and any special skills needed.

2Candidate Selection

We identify potential candidates from our database who match your requirements and conduct preliminary screenings.

3Verification

We verify all certifications, qualifications, and experience, and conduct background checks to ensure the highest standards.

4Client Review

We present you with qualified candidates for your review and approval, including comprehensive profiles and documentation.

5Deployment

Once approved, we handle all logistics to ensure the personnel arrive at your vessel on schedule, including travel arrangements, visas, and any required documentation.

Our Quality Assurance

At Africa Link, we maintain rigorous quality assurance standards for all personnel we provide:

  • Thorough vetting of qualifications and experience
  • Verification of all certificates and licenses
  • Comprehensive background checks
  • Regular performance evaluations
  • Compliance with international maritime standards and regulations
  • Ongoing training and certification updates
