Transit Services

Streamlining customs clearance and transit logistics across Africa

Our Transit Services

At AfricaLink, we understand that navigating the complexities of customs clearance and transit logistics in Africa can be challenging. Our dedicated transit services team provides expert guidance and comprehensive solutions to ensure your cargo moves smoothly across borders and to its final destination.

With extensive knowledge of regional customs regulations and strong relationships with local authorities, we help eliminate delays, reduce costs, and ensure compliance throughout the transit process.

Customs Clearance Services

Documentation Management

We handle all required documentation for customs clearance, ensuring accuracy and compliance with local regulations:

  • Bills of lading preparation and management
  • Commercial invoice verification
  • Certificate of origin documentation
  • Import/export license coordination
  • Customs declaration forms

Customs Broker Services

Our licensed customs brokers represent your interests and facilitate efficient clearance:

  • Classification of goods for proper tariff applications
  • Duty and tax calculation and payment
  • Liaison with customs officials
  • Regulatory compliance assurance
  • Expedited clearance services

Regulatory Compliance

We ensure your shipments meet all regulatory requirements:

  • Trade compliance audits
  • Regulatory updates and advisory services
  • Specialized permits acquisition
  • Health and safety compliance
  • Environmental regulation adherence

Duty Optimization

We help minimize duty expenses through legal optimization strategies:

  • Tariff classification analysis
  • Free trade agreement utilization
  • Bonded warehouse solutions
  • Duty drawback and recovery services
  • Strategic import planning

Transit Logistics

Our transit logistics services provide end-to-end solutions for moving your cargo efficiently through Africa:

  • Multi-modal Transportation: Coordination of sea, air, rail, and road transport for seamless cargo movement
  • Route Optimization: Strategic planning to identify the most efficient and cost-effective transport routes
  • Cross-border Management: Expert handling of border crossings and transit formalities
  • Cargo Tracking: Real-time monitoring and status updates on your shipments
  • Risk Management: Comprehensive strategies to mitigate transit risks and ensure security

Regional Expertise

Our transit services are strengthened by our deep regional expertise across Africa:

  • West African customs procedures and regulations
  • East African Community (EAC) customs union protocols
  • Southern African Development Community (SADC) trade agreements
  • North African border and port operations
  • African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) implementation
  • Country-specific import/export requirements
